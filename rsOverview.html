How to test:
  • Scroll and resize your browser (make sure browser scroll bars are visible)
  • Drag and move the viewport (blue rectangle)
  • Click on the red area for fast scroll

Note: If you are using IE 6 or below, you might see the plugin control might not stay fixed when page scrolls. This is a know issue related to a lack of support for fixed elements in IE. A workaround for this will be available soon.
